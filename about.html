About Food Rescue Network

Together, we fight hunger, reduce waste, and create a sustainable future.

Our Mission

We aim to bridge the gap between surplus food and those in need. By connecting donors, NGOs, and volunteers, we transform potential waste into meals and hope for communities.

Our Vision

We envision a world where no food goes to waste and no person goes hungry. Through technology, collaboration, and awareness, we work towards a sustainable food ecosystem.

Our Journey

Our Journey

It Started at Home

We didn’t want to throw away leftovers from our own kitchen — that was the seed of an idea.

Community Functions

We encouraged people at family events and gatherings not to waste food and started sharing it.

Partnering with Restaurants

We expanded by helping restaurants and businesses rescue surplus food instead of discarding it.

Our Goal: Zero Food Waste

Our journey continues with one mission — to build a world where no good food goes to waste.
