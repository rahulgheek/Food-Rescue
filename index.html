A Simple Path to Zero Waste

Our streamlined process makes it effortless to donate surplus food and make a difference.

1. Donors List Surplus

A business with extra food lists it on our platform in minutes.

2. Charities Get Notified

Nearby non-profits and food banks receive an instant alert.

3. Food is Collected

A volunteer driver or charity member picks up the donation.

4. Food Reaches People

The rescued food is distributed to families and individuals in need.
